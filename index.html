The Importance of Piano Health System

Changing weather affects the humidity level – especially in tropical areas – that could damage your piano in which most of its parts are made out of wood. Piano Health System is a tool to control the humidity level approximately in areas that are acceptable and comfortable for your piano.

Voicing

Voicing is the adjustment of piano’s tone or quality of sound. Tone can be changed without affecting the pitch. Only a skilled piano technician can voice a piano to change its tonal personality from mellow to bright or robust to delicate. The degree of change possible depends upon the piano’s design and condition.

Care & Repair

A piano requires not only regular tuning, but attention to the proper operation of its mechanical parts. Just because all the keys work doesn’t mean the piano is in good shape. Over time, felts wear down or become compressed and need replacement. The action needs periodic adjustment, called “regulation”.

Tuning

Tuning is the adjustment of the tension of all of your piano’s 220 (or more) strings to the correct pitch or frequency. This ensures that notes played in a musical interval (octaves, chords, etc.) will sound in harmony.
